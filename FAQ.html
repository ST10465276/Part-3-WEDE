FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

What is Thina Mutsila Legal?

TM Legal is an non-profit organisation founded by Thinavhuyo Mutsila,a LLB graduate who aimes to provide free legal advice and assistance to underprivileged and disadvantaged members of the community.

Can students apply for the volunteering?

Yes. We welcome all interested candidates to volunteer at TM Legal.Particularly, law students are encouraged to apply as volunteering provides valuable practical experience and an opportunity to contribute to community legal support.

What type of legal matters do you handle?

We mainly focus on:

  • Community education and legal awareness
  • Human rights and constitutional issues
  • But we can also assist in other legal matters, to the best of our capabilities

Do you provide legal representation in court?

No,TM Legal does not represent you in court but we do offer legal advice, referral to pro bono attorneys and mandated organizations where necessary.

Do you offer paying positions to volunteering legal students ?

Currently,TM legal being a small organisation does not have the capacity to offer paying positions but as we grow, hopefully with more funding will create oppportunites for our upcoming students

How can I donate to support your mission?

Donations can be made via:

  • Bank transfer, our banking details are provided under the "Donations" link
  • Or you are free to come In-person at our office

    • Every contribution helps us continue to provide legal aid to those in need

What documentation do I need to provide to receive assistance?

Depending on your case, all required documentation will be discussed and confirmed to the client during the consultation.

Do you offer your services only online?

No,we offer our services both online and in-person, but preferrably in-person in order to get a better feel of the case at hand
